Why is the sky blue? Why does the earth orbit the sun? Why does copper transmit electricity so well? These all seem like perfectly reasonable questions to ask. Questions that we, as physicists can answer. Yet, I entitled this post the impossibility of why. In general, questions about why are not good questions for physicists More accurately we answer questions about how. Or what. What phenomena causes use to see the sky as blue?
